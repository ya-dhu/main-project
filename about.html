About Us

History

Fox Store was founded in 1969 with a vision to provide anime enthusiasts with a curated collection of high-quality figures. Over the years, we've grown to become a go-to destination for anime figure collectors.

Motivation

Our motivation is fueled by a passion for anime and the desire to bring the beloved characters to life through meticulously crafted figures. We aim to capture the essence and spirit of each character in every piece we offer.

Founders

Fox Store was founded by Naruto and Kakashi. Their shared love for anime and a commitment to quality inspired the creation of a store that caters to the discerning tastes of anime figure enthusiasts.

First Store Location

The first Fox Store opened its doors in Tokyo in 1969. It started as a small shop dedicated to providing a unique and immersive shopping experience for anime fans.

Current Headquarters Location

Our current headquarters is located in New York, USA. From our humble beginnings, we've expanded our operations while staying true to our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
